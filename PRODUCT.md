# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Organizers create events, share invitations, manage guest lists, and review RSVP status. Guests open unlisted invitation links, understand essential event details, and respond with minimal friction, often on a mobile device.

## Product Purpose

Invite App provides the smallest reliable workflow for creating and managing invitations and collecting RSVPs. Success means organizers can understand attendance at a glance while guests can respond quickly without unnecessary accounts, data collection, or feature complexity.

## Brand Personality

Clear, calm, and trustworthy. The product should feel modern and focused while remaining approachable for occasional users who are planning a meaningful event rather than operating specialist software.

## Anti-references

- Feature-heavy event platforms that obscure core tasks behind payments, gift lists, QR codes, check-in, integrations, or configuration.
- Generic marketing pages that prioritize decorative effects over event and RSVP workflows.
- Overbuilt design systems, novel controls, and speculative abstractions that slow MVP learning.
- Interfaces that expose private organizer or guest data, rely on color alone, or make mobile RSVP completion difficult.

## Design Principles

- Keep the next task obvious and remove everything that does not support it.
- Make public RSVP paths faster and simpler than organizer workflows.
- Use familiar interaction patterns and consistent domain language.
- Communicate status, validation, loading, empty, and error states explicitly.
- Build only what the current MVP needs, with clear seams for validated change.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

The interface is mobile-first, keyboard accessible, screen-reader friendly, and usable at 200% zoom. Text and controls maintain WCAG AA contrast, status never depends on color alone, focus is always visible, touch targets are practical, and all motion respects reduced-motion preferences.
